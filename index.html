Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Beam

What is Beam?
Beam is a professional quoting tool designed for freelancers, indie hackers, and small teams. Create beautiful PDF quotes in under 60 seconds, send them via SMS, and track when customers view and approve them. No more spending hours in Word or Excel.
Who is Beam for?
Beam is built for plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, carpenters, painters, handymen, and any trade professional who needs to send professional quotes quickly. It's also great for freelancers and small service businesses who want to look professional without the hassle.
How much does Beam cost?
Beam uses a freemium model. Start with our Free plan — 5 quotes per month, PDF generation, and basic templates at no cost. When you're ready for more, upgrade to Pro at £29/month for unlimited quotes, SMS delivery, premium templates, custom branding, and more.
When will Beam launch?
Beam is currently in active development with 500+ contractors already using the beta. Sign up for early access to be the first to know when we launch fully — and lock in special early-bird pricing.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're UK-based and fully GDPR compliant. Your customer data belongs to you — we never sell it or use it for anything other than providing the service.
Can I export my data?
Yes! We believe in data portability. You can export all your quotes, customer data, and templates at any time — even on the free plan. We don't hold your data hostage if you decide to leave.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 14-day free trial on Pro so you can try everything before paying. If you're not satisfied after subscribing, contact us within 30 days for a full refund — no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.
